How to join the course

  1. Tell us something about yourself by filling in this Form.
  2. Jump in and start the lessons in PY4E site.
  3. Sign up on this page to log your progress by clicking on the Register or Login button above or simply click here.
  4. Share your progress and actively collaborate in Discord. We will wait for you there.

Some key points about this course

  • The course is designed for complete beginners particularly those with zero coding knowledge.
  • This website is solely used just for progress tracking purposes.
  • Just follow Dr Chucks PY4E lessons and use the book as a complementing aid in your learning and update your progress here every time you finish a chapter.
  • It is important to engage in Discord so we can handhold each other in this journey.
  • One of the steps is to explore an open dataset. If you explore an Australian dataset, we can publish your work on Code.Sydney's Portfolios Page to be part of your growing portfolio.
  • Get a taste of what coding is all about. It might help you become a super-productive, rockstar 10x employee at work, or even lead to a career switch.
  • Have fun, take everything easy and keep learning!
