How to join the course

  1. Sign up on this page by clicking on the Register or Login button above or simply click here.
  2. Jump in and start the lessons in PY4E site.
  3. Important! Share your progress and actively collaborate in Discord. We will wait for you there.
  4. Not required but if you like to include your code in our common GitHub repo.
  5. Tell us something about yourself by filling in this Form

Some key points about this course

  • The course is designed for complete beginners particularly those which zero coding knowledge.
  • This website is solely used just for progress tracking purposes.
  • Just follow Dr Chucks PY4E lessons and use the book as a complementing aid in your learning and update your progress here every time you finish a chapter.
  • It is important to engage in Discord so we can handhold each other in this journey.
  • Capstone project is to explore an open dataset. If you explore an Australian dataset, we can publish your work on Code.Sydney's Portfolios Page to be part of your growing portfolio.
  • Have fun, take everything easy and keep learning!
