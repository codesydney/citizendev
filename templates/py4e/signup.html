Courses from Prof. Charles Severance

We will be using free Python and Django courses from Professor Charles Severance, also known as Dr. Chuck, is a well-known figure in the field of computer science and online education. He has made significant contributions to the development of educational platforms and resources, particularly in programming and web technologies.

Dr. Chuck has been involved in various educational initiatives, including creating and teaching online courses on platforms like Coursera, edX, and Sakai. He is particularly recognised for his work in the area of Python programming, and his courses have attracted a wide audience of learners from around the world.

In addition to his teaching and educational efforts, Dr. Chuck has been involved in research, writing, and speaking engagements focused on topics such as web development, learning analytics, and online education. Overall, Professor Charles Severance is a prominent figure in the intersection of computer science and education, known for his contributions to both fields.
